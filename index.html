What We Offer

Sizar School is an English medium Day-Boarding School, offering a first class, affordable education for boys and girls with classes Play-Group to Viii std. We are able to offer individual attention and able to appreciate the unique qualities of each and every child, ensuring every step of their education is tailored to meet their needs.

the whole purpose of education is to turn mirrors into windows

Sizar School was established in 2001 as a Day-Boarding upper primary school. Now in the hands of God Almighty today it is one of the outstanding English Medium Co-Educational School of Jaipur which is providing good education to a large number of students having well educated and dedicated staff. In a good spacious building having sufficient furniture and all necessaries we have taken upon us the whole burden of teaching leaving least on the part of the Parents. Parents are not given the problem of home work being aware of the fact of their busy life.
We are proud of having done "Much in Little" as is our motto. Teaching methods uphold traditional values, whilst encompassing new ideas and initiatives to meet the needs of children growing up in an ever changing world. We aim to nurture our children in an atmosphere of one large family, where there is a strong sense of community and where children feel happy, safe and secure. In achieving this, our children flourish in all areas of their development. There is a high emphasis on both academic achievement and educating the whole child to prepare them for life as adults and the wider world.

Who We Are

We believes that education transforms lives, and that transformed people can make a difference in their societies and in the world by working for tolerance, understanding, justice, equity and compassion. About Us

Our Past

The extract of Education is gaining self confidence which is relaxing as there is no strain or stress when one is self-confident.Education being a must and a part of life, to get rid from the course of illiteracy, as the standard of living, science and technology, ways of studying, toughness of getting job to survive in the world etc. are fast running to an inevitable height, we should be aware of the importance of Education.
Keeping all these things in mind the Manoj Public Secondary School Educational Society was formed under the Chairmanship of Mr. R.N.Choudhary with the guidance and help of Mrs. Tara Chaudhary in 1981 to cater the educational needs of the society.

Our Present

Now in the hands of God Almighty today it is one of the outstanding English/Hindi Medium Co-Educational School of Jaipur which is providing good education to a large number of students having well educated and dedicated staff. In a good spacious building having sufficient furniture and all necessaries. And we are proud to say that these all things made us capable to give 100% Result in all Board Exams from last 4 years.
Now we have adopted a new system of education as it is a day Boarding institution, have taken upon us the whole burden of teaching leaving least on the part of the Parents. Parents are not given the problem of home work being aware of the fact of their busy life. We are proud of having done "Much in Little" as is our motto.

Our Aims

The School aims at the total formation of the young. They are to be adults with good habits, good character and moral values needed for a life that is socially meaningful and productive. We make them aim to rise into the highest peak of education to succeed in the further struggle of life. All these days we have tried to give them good education, good teaching and a healthy atmosphere including extra curricular activities such as dance, skating, gymnastics etc.
The playground in many ways is an extension of the class-room. It educates the hold in coping with different situations. The child learns self-discipline by obeying the rules of the games. He strives to overcome his own handicaps and short comings and in doing so he earns self confidence. He learns to co-operate, to take defeat gracefully and to persevere for excellence and more over to appreciate skills and abilities that matter more than caste and creed.
